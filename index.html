HeartMeter

The HeartMeter is an assessment tool that measures the level of engagement, energy and enthusiasm in businesses. The tool especially targets the positive and negative emotional associations employees have to their work and their well being, and perceives it in the context of the company employees - customer satisfaction, results and growth.

How does it work?
In a good corporate culture employees feel there is engagement, opportunity and room for good vertical and horizontal co-creation. The job gives and creates meaning, growth and development, and employees believe they are part of a bigger vision.
We will find how the employees themselves feel about their positions, their leaders, and the companies they are employed in. This is to illustrate the degree of impact job satisfaction, growth and development in an engaged work culture have on innovation, growth and results.
